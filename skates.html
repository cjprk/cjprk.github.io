In Soft Tech Sublimate, a pair of Softec skates is embedded in a block of ice. Laden with contact microphones, the bucket beneath amplifies the falling droplets, distorting the backing music. Viewers are confronted with a space that oscillates between calm and calamitous.

My installation/sculptural work is concerned with questions of ecology, death and data.
