soft tech

2018

wood block, chain, skates, ice, bucket, contact mic

in soft tech, a pair of skates is embedded in a block of ice. the sounds of falling droplets are amplified and modulated, producing a space that oscillates between calm and calamitous.

exhibited at the granoff 2017 spring arts festival in providence, rhode island.
