excerpts from my Modern Culture and Media Senior Honors Thesis, an experimental documentary project that was screened, read and performed at Brown University in December 2017.

Raised in Hong Kong and educated between England and the US, I have had to reckon with cultural outsiderness from different climates and altitudes. An observer and listener, I derive inspiration from following and forecasting trends in visual culture. I am empowered by the prospect of harnessing new technologies to share stories and ideas with the world. tldr; I am constantly in search of the best way to really reach someone.
