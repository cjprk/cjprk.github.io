廢青, 2017

a derivation of "頹廢青年" or "wasted youth", the term 廢青 (fai cing) emerged more recently as hong kong's brand of millennial hate. a reclamation of the pejorative "廢青", this film pries open the cantonese colloquialism, re-distributing its connotations spanning color, flora, decay and abandonment.

the end of the world does not distinguish between plant life and digital detritus. an exemplar of the city as interface, hong kong provides the setting for this film as a reverse-terraforming project, and an opportunity to think about growing out of the city's ruins.

loss/lessness, 2017

the devices we entrust with our memories age as we do. this film follows the protracted death of an iPhone 6s. registering its revival on the same DV-tape used to film my grandmother's funeral ceremony, loss/lessness is an attempt to overcome the life-death threshold and stand against the current of time.
