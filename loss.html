further video work can be found below:

i) 廢青, 2019

a derivation of "頹廢青年" ("wasted youth"), the pejorative 廢青 (fai cing) encapsulates hong kong's brand of milennial disdain. a reclamation of the pejorative "廢青", this film pries open the colloquialism, re-distributing its connotations that span color, flora, decay and abandonment.

the end of the world does not distinguish between plant life and digital detritus. an exemplar of the city as interface, hong kong provides the setting for this reverse-terraforming project, and an opportunity to think about growing out of the city's ruins.

ii) loss/lessness, 2018

the devices we entrust with our memories age as we do. this film follows the protracted death of an iPhone 6s. recording its death and revival on the same DV-tape that was used to film my grandmother's funeral ceremony, loss/lessness presents an attempt to stand against the current of time
